Paste text

Two ways to turn a BRD into requirements

Extract requirements (no AI): runs a keyword rule engine directly on the text in the box — works best when it's already roughly one requirement per line. Analyze BRD with AI: sends the raw, messy document text to your configured AI provider (see Export tab) first, which rewrites it into clean, atomic, numbered requirement lines and puts the result back in the box — then click "Extract requirements" as normal to classify and add them. Each requirement is scanned for keywords and classified into a module category (Authentication, CRUD, Search, Payment, Calculation, Uploads, Date Logic, Access Control, Notifications, API, etc), with concrete details — field names, numeric limits — pulled out of the wording to ground the generated test data.
